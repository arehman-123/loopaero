Loop aero

ABOUT US:

LoopAero.com is part of Loop Aerospace, an FAA Parts Manufacturing Approved (FAA-PMA) company making aircraft parts since 1995 and one of the first in California to obtain FAA approval under ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿14 CFR 21.137(o)﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿ to sign off its own manufactured parts with the FAA Form 8130-3, an approval given only to companies with a quality system and the appropriate training procedures to receive this special﻿﻿ approval﻿﻿.

Bert Brache - CEO and Founder

Started as an aircraft fueler at Burbank airport California in 1975. Obtained his aircraft mechanic's licenses (Airframe & Powerplant) and graduated from Los Angeles Trade Technical College in 1978 with an Associates Degree and on the Dean's Honor List. Worked as an avionics installer initially for Tiger Air at the Burbank airport and eventually worked full-time as a jet mechanic on corporate aircraft. Hired by Continental Airlines at LAX to do heavy maintenance on Boeing 727 and McDonald Douglas DC-10 and also obtained Inspection Authorization from the FAA in 1980. Received first FAA Part Manufacturing Approval in February 1995 at the Van Nuys Airport California and also pilot’s license later in 1995. Moved company to Santa Clarita, California with FAA approval August 2016.

Chris Handley - Director of Regulatory Affairs / Instructor

Formerly an Aviation Safety Inspector (ASI) with the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) from September 2006 – June 2014 (7 years 10 months) Van Nuys California. Prior to the FAA worked as Quality Manager - R&D Engineer - Conformity DMIR for Hartzell Propeller Inc. (Electrofilm) September 1994 – September 2007 (13 years 1 month) Valencia, California. Joined the team at Loop Aerospace as the Director of Regulatory Affairs where he, along with Bert Brache and Gary Morgan, obtained the quality procedures necessary to receive the FAA's approval to sign off its own parts with the FAA 8130-3 form per 14 CFR 21.137(o).

Gary Crosslin - Office Administrator

Began his interest in the aerospace industry by enlisting in the US Air Force at age 18 where he studied meteorology and spent 3 years stationed overseas in Germany as a Weather Observer on the flight line . Studies took him to the legal field as a Paralegal for 13 years, and obtained sales and administrative experience working 11 years with the Neptune Society, the Home Improvement Industry and as a Substance Abuse Counselor. Gary C returned to the aerospace industry accepting a position as materials handler at Net Jets. And is currently the Office Administrator at Loop Aerospace, where he helps maintain quality production standards for the FAA-PMA manufacturing company.

Gary Morgan - Quality Manager

Our most experienced staff member at Loop Aerospace with over 40 years in the aerospace industry. Gary M oversees the quality and engineering side of Loop while still active as the Director of Aircraft Maintence for a corporate jet operator and a company operating 2 helicopters. Gary M is also a Loop PMA Partner who brought the Hawker bracket assembly and rub strips through the FAA Parts Manufacturing Approval process and now enjoys the benefits of getting paid every time these parts sell at www.LoopPMA.com.
